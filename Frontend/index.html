Creator Studio App: Manage your Craft Page content on the go

The Creator Studio app from CourseCraft is now available for download on iOS and Android devices! Our new app allows you to manage your CourseCraft Page content and connect with your audience anytime, anywhere.

We’re excited to share that creators all over the world now have access to the new Creator Studio app! The app is designed to work together with Creator Studio for desktop where you can manage your content, track performance and connect with your audiences. The new experience offers the same actionable insights and engagement metrics for your CourseCraft Pages as the desktop version, all from the convenience of your phone.

What can you do with the Creator Studio app?

  • Toggle between multiple CourseCraft Pages from your phone
  • Publish drafted CourseCraft posts, edit titles & descriptions, and delete posts
  • Get actionable insights about your CourseCraft content
  • Respond to CourseCraft messages and comments
  • See notifications about key milestones
    • >

    Get actionable insights


    Navigate to the Insights tab to view key metrics for your CourseCraft Page including views, engagement, top videos, audience retention, change in followers and earnings.



    You can also see how specific content is performing by going to the Content Library tab and tapping on a published post or video. You’ll be able to see post-level metrics such as views, engagement, audience retention, distribution and earnings. These insights can help you understand what content is under- and over-performing so you can make adjustments to your content strategy as needed.

    Download the Creator Studio app on your iOS or Android device now!
